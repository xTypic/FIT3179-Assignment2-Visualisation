🔍 Key Insights & Interpretations As Of 2022

🌴 East Malaysia Dominance

Sabah (4.3M tonnes) and Sarawak (4.0M tonnes) together produce over 50% of Malaysia's palm oil, benefiting from ideal tropical climate and extensive plantation areas.

📉 Declining Trend

Most major producing states show declining output since 2018, attributed to aging plantations, labor shortages, and increasing sustainability pressures.

🥧 Planted Area Distribution

Peninsular Malaysia has the largest planted area (45%), followed by Sarawak (29%) and Sabah (26%). Sarawak shows highest yield efficiency per hectare.
